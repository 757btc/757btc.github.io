**Meetup May 9th 2026 - 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM at [Andy Boys Pizza](https://andyboyspizza.net/?utm_source=google), 3916 Portsmouth Blvd A4, Chesapeake, VA 23321**

--- 
Please join us at 4:00pm for a family friendly meetup with 757BTC at our local pizza shop that accepts Bitcoin! One of our members will be presenting his research on measuring when Bitcoin forks succeed and when they become contentious and risky. This research will be presented at Wyoming Universities Bitcoin Reseasrch Institute in July and the local community gets a preview of the results and provide feedback. All are welcome even if you are new to Bitcoin come out to meet some of the local Bitcoin community an experience the fun and joy when you pay for Pizza peer to peer with Bitcoin.
