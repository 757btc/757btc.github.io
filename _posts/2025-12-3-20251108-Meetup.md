**Meetup December 13th 2025 - 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM at [Beach Montessori Christian Academy](https://beachmontessorichristianacademy.com/), 1101 Eaglewood Dr in Virginia Beach**

---
Its that time of year where you need to shop for you last minute Christams gifts. Why not support your local circular Bitcoin economy and get some great gifts. The 757btc and Virginia Beach meetup groups will be hosting a Christmas market with vendors that accept Bitcoin. All interested merchants can register at this link: [Bitcoin Market Registration](https://757btc.org/register/)

Join the Hampton Roads Bitcoiners in Virginia Beach for great conversation and fellowship. The meetup will have plenty of hands on activities for everyone to try. Bitcoin exchange demonstrations and how to's for everyone interested no matter what your skill level. Bring your curious friends and family members and lets build the Bitcoin community. Hope to see you all there!
