**Meetup January 10th 2026 - 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at [Mile Zero Marine](https://milezeromarine.com/), 115 High Street Portsmouth, VA 23704**

--- 
Please join us at 2:00pm for a family friendly meetup with 757BTC to ring in the new year and discuss all things bitcoin & freedom tech. Can you believe that it has be 17 years since Satoshi Nakamoto mined the first block on the Bitcoin blockchain!? 

![alt text](/assets/img/genesisBlockSlide.png)

We will discuss all that the meetup has accomplished in 2025 and where we plan to go in 2026!

Some members are bringing food and drink to share. This is not required but much appreciated! We will be meeting at Mile Zero Marine, a small business in downtown Portsmouth run by a local Bitcoiner. Seating is limited, so if you have a camp chair it may be advisable to bring it.
