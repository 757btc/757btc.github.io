**Meetup March 14th 2026 - 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at [Mile Zero Marine](https://milezeromarine.com/), 115 High Street Portsmouth, VA 23704**

---

This month we're doing something different. Less price talk, more building.

### Bitcoin LARP

Ever wondered what actually happens when you send a transaction? We're going to find out — by acting it out. Participants will take on roles in the Bitcoin network: nodes, miners, wallets, the mempool. You'll physically move transactions through the system and see how consensus works with your own eyes (and feet).

This is based on an open source framework from BTC++ designed for community groups to run. No technical background required — that's the whole point. If you've been trying to explain Bitcoin to someone and the whiteboard isn't cutting it, bring them to this.

### Freedom Tech Stack

Short presentation on the layers of freedom tech — what it is, why it matters, and how the tools fit together. We'll cover how open source software forms the foundation and why that matters for sovereignty.

### Vibe Coding & AI Agents

After talking about open source, we'll show it in action. Live demo of AI-assisted software development (yes, "vibe coding" is what the kids are calling it) and a look at AI agents — what they can do, how to build with them, and why you don't need to be a developer to start.

Bring a laptop if you want to follow along.
